14. 2026-04-09 OpenVINO real-model blocker 更新
- 当前错误仍是:
  - `aarch64 + openvino 2026.1.0`
  - CPU plugin:
    - `Supported Reduce executor is not found`
- 新定位:
  - 原始 ONNX 内有 `8` 个 `GlobalAveragePool`
  - OpenVINO 转换后这些会落成 `ReduceMean`
  - 把这 `8` 个 `GlobalAveragePool` 静态改写成等价 `AveragePool` 后
    - `Reduce` blocker 会消失
    - 但马上出现新的 blocker:
      - `Supported Interpolate executor is not found`
- 结论:
  - `OpenVINO real-model` 现在不是单点脚本 bug
  - 而是当前 ARM CPU plugin 对这张 DBNet 图存在多处 executor 缺口
  - 不要再误判成“只差一个 ReduceMean 修补”
