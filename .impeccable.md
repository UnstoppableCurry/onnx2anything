## Design Context

### Users
Developers and model users converting ONNX models in the browser. They want a quick, reliable path from upload to downloadable output without reading implementation details.

### Brand Personality
Clear, calm, and practical. The interface should feel guided, confident, and a little lighter than a typical engineering tool.

### Aesthetic Direction
Minimal and product-like. Fewer words, simpler section titles, softer hierarchy, and smooth state changes. Avoid heavy technical framing and overly dense control panels.

### Design Principles
- Lead with the next action, not the implementation detail.
- Keep language short, human, and specific.
- Make progress and results feel like part of one guided flow.
- Use motion and scrolling to reduce friction, not to decorate.
- Prefer progressive disclosure over showing every advanced control at once.
